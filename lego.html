Learning Solidwork's parts and Assembly through lego design project

The objective was to model at least 2 parts in 2 weeks that can be assembled. Solidword's drawing feature will then be used to develop dimensioned drawings of a three view and isometric drawing for each part, an isometric drawing of the assembly, and the exploded view of the assembly.The three parts that I chose were the torso, head and legs of a lego figure as at the time that is what an assembly most resembled.This project taught me to properly interface with Solidworks by utilizing tools like mirroring features, loft, and extrude cut by surfaces. Planes were also necessary.
