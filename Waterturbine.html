Renewable Energy Project- Water Turbine Rapid Prototyping

After brainstorming the design that utilizes the Bernoulli Principle for inlet and outlet areas to have a greater pressure and velocity of flow to spin the turbines. The turbines then spin the internal PVC pipe that has brass rings that are seated with ball bearings on either end to allow for rotation. By rapidly rotating with the help of the pressed ball bearings, the magnets that are on the PVC pipe rotate within the external copper coils,creating an alternating electrical current. This is then converted to DC current using full-wave rectification. A DC Voltage booster was then used to achieve the voltage output necessary.

The majority of materials used were manufactured in-house or ordered. The brass rings were made from a brass block using the milling machine, the PVC pipe cut using a pipe cutting machine,and the caps and turbine 3-D printed using slicer software and Prusa 3D printers.
